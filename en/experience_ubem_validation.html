Dynamic UBEM calibration, validation & diagnostics framework

Page ID
experience_ubem_validation

Context

Urban building energy models are increasingly used to analyse the energy performance of buildings at the urban scale, particularly for renovation, the integration of renewable energies and demand-side management. However, the validation of these models still relies mainly on global indicators, which are not well suited to dynamic use cases and imperfect urban-scale measurements data. My thesis addresses this issue by proposing a decision-oriented validation approach that takes into account data constraints, the gap between the validation domain and the application domain, and the time scales of interest.
